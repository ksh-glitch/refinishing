03 — On CostStraight answers

Asked, and answered

What does restoration cost?

How much does it cost to refinish a dining table?

It depends on size, condition and the finish you choose — which is why we quote from photographs rather than a price sheet. As a very rough national range, a full strip-and-refinish of a dining table runs from several hundred dollars up past a thousand for large or damaged pieces. Send photos and we’ll give you a real number, quickly and for free.

What drives the price of reupholstery?

Mostly the fabric you choose and the state of the frame underneath. A sound frame with simple lines costs far less than a piece that needs re-gluing, new webbing and springs. We’ll tell you honestly when a frame deserves the investment — and when it doesn’t.

Is the estimate really free?

Yes — the estimate is free, there is no obligation, and we take no payment up front. You pay on delivery, when you’ve seen the piece and you’re more than satisfied.

Does restoring an antique hurt its value?

Done wrong, it can — which is why every step here is by hand, with reversible, period-appropriate techniques that preserve the original colors and tonalities of the wood. If a piece is better served by conservation than refinishing, we’ll say so.
