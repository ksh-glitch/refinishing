Our story · Washington DC · MD · VA

A family workshop, est. 1991.

One bench, one standard, thirty-five years. This is the story of the studio that restores fine furniture and antiques — every step by hand — for households across Washington DC, Maryland and Virginia.

01 — The StoryNew York, 1991 → Sterling, 1996

Family owned since 1991

Begun in New York, at home in Virginia.

Antiques & Furniture Restoration Inc was founded in New York City in 1991. In 1996 the workshop moved to Sterling, Virginia, where our 3,000-square-foot shop handles complete repair and refinishing — French polishing, color matching, piece replacement, re-gluing, upholstery and repair.

The skills at the bench have been earned hands-on, under the guidance of Yasser Haridi and his staff of local craftsmen and experts. Every step in the total process — from the stripping to the final finish — is done by hand, with environmentally safe and oil-based products chosen to preserve the original colors, tonalities and warmth of the wood.

The focus never changes: maintain the look and artistic value of your fine furniture, and retain the value and integrity of the antique underneath.

Insurance claims

We handle insurance claims for fire, smoke and water damage — documenting the piece, restoring it, and protecting its worth after the worst has happened.

Commercial service

From touch-up to full restoration, we understand the specialized needs of the commercial client: delivery and moving companies, re-location companies, hotels, hospitals, retirement homes, offices, banks and more — expert quality service at competitive rates.

05 — QuestionsAsked at the bench

Before you call

Asked, and answered.

How do I get an estimate?

Send a few photos and a short description of the work to info@refinishing.org or through our free estimate form — we reply quickly with an honest quote. Estimates are always free.

Do you pick up and deliver?

Yes — pick-up and delivery are free throughout Washington DC, Maryland and Virginia. Clients regularly tell us how much this matters; we come to your home for both ends of the job.

Do you take payment up front?

No. We don’t take money up front — we’re paid on delivery, when we’re sure you’re more than satisfied with the work.

Angie’s List Super Service Award, year after year · Rated Washington’s best antique restoration

How do you strip old finishes?

Entirely by hand. We never use acids, high-pressure water or dipping tanks — the piece comes down to bare wood the slow way, so fragile veneer, inlay and joinery survive.

Can I choose the stain myself?

Yes — and on your actual piece, not a sample card. Once the wood is stripped and sanded, you visit the shop and we apply as many stain samples as it takes until you’re happy. If you can’t make it to Sterling, we’ll match a stain from photographs of the furniture you want to coordinate with.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes. We handle insurance claims for fire, smoke and water damage to fine furniture and antiques.

Do you work with businesses?

We do — from touch-up to full restoration for delivery and moving companies, re-location companies, hotels, hospitals, retirement homes, offices, banks and more, at competitive rates.
