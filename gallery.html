The visual archive · DC · MD · VA

The plates.

Since 1991 the shop has kept one habit without fail: every piece is photographed three times — the day it arrives, stripped by hand to bare wood, and fully restored. No staging, no retouching. What follows is that record, kept for heirlooms from across Washington DC, Maryland and Virginia.

01 — The Three-Step ShowcaseAs received · Stripped · Restored

The shop tradition

Three photographs, every piece.

The middle photograph is the honest one. Stripped to bare wood, a piece hides nothing — and neither do we. It is the step that decides whether a finish lasts a season or a generation.
